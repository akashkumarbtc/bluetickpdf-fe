Privacy Policy

At BluetickPDF, we take your privacy and the security of your information very seriously. This privacy policy section aims to provide you with a clear understanding of how we handle your data when you use our generative AI app and its services.

Collection and Use of Information

When you use BluetickPDF, we utilize Google API services and OAuth3 for authentication. We do not store any user data in our own database. Instead, we rely on the secure infrastructure provided by Google to handle user authentication and access permissions.

Authentication

When you choose to connect your Google account with BluetickPDF, we use OAuth3 to establish a secure connection. This allows us to access the necessary data within your Google account to read and process the PDF files you provide. We do not store any login credentials or personal information associated with your Google account.

PDF Processing

BluetickPDF's primary function is to read and analyze PDF files provided by users. When you upload a PDF, it is temporarily stored in a secure manner to process your queries and generate relevant results. Once the processing is complete, the PDF file is automatically deleted from our system.

Data Storage

We do not retain any user data or PDF files on our servers after processing. All data is deleted within a reasonable timeframe once it is no longer needed for the functioning of the app.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information and have implemented appropriate measures to protect it. We adhere to industry-standard security practices and use encryption techniques to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of the data during transmission and storage.

Third-Party Services

BluetickPDF relies on Google API services for authentication and access to your PDF files. While we take necessary precautions to ensure the security of your data within our app, we encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how they handle your information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy section from time to time to reflect any changes in our practices or in compliance with legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our data handling practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us at contact@bluetickpdf.com. We will be happy to assist you and address your concerns.
